THE WAY SOCIALIZING SHOULD BE

Here, we help you build connections that last a lifetime.

What is Orbt?

Orbt brings people together over coffee, brunch, and dinner. We curate real-world group events so you can meet great people without the awkwardness.

No swiping. No small talk. Just good energy, good food, and the right mix of people.

Founder’s Note

Social apps used to feel social. Now they’re noisy, performative, and exhausting.

We built Orbt to make connection simple again. Whether it's over a cup of coffee or a shared table at dinner, Orbt is about being present with the right people.
